About Brian

Brian is a Boston-based Software Engineer. He graduated with a B.A. in Math and Computer Science from Boston University in 2017. Since June 2017, Brian has worked at Optum, the technology arm of UHG. During his time at Optum, Brian has worked on a range of projects from large enterprise claims portals to small APIs based off NLP Research that he has started himself. He has experience working everywhere on the technology stack, having worked on the back end, front end, CI/CD and ML parts of given projects.
